Links

Links are clickable references that take you from one place to another online. Click one and your browser jumps to a new webpage, file, email draft, or section of the same page. They’re the reason the internet is a “web” instead of just isolated documents.

Syntax of Links in HTML

The syntax for links is: <a href="URL">link text</a>

href

It stands for Hypertext Reference. It's an attribute that tells the link where to go.

Types of Links in HTML

  • External link: W3schools <a> tags
  • Internal link: Contact Us
  • Anchor link: Back to Top
  • Download link: Download Notes

Common Website Examples:

  • Spotify.com
  • Wikipedia.com
  • YouTube.com
  • ChatGPT.com
