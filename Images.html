Inserting Images in HTML

To insert images in HTML,we use the <img> tag.

Syntax for the <img>Tag

<img src="Pathname" alt="alt name" width="" height="" loading="eager/lazy">

NOTE* Width and Height are not necessary but when Loading mode is "lazy" then it has to be specified.

Lazy loading means it tells the browser to load the image when we are scrolling and getting near it.

Eager loading means it tells the browser to load the image as soon as we open the webpage.

Gen 1 Legendary Pokémons

Today we are ging to know about the gen 1 legendary pokémons

The legendary Birds

  • Articuno

    Articuno is an Ice/Flying type Legendary Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. Articuno has no known evolutionary line, and is one of the three Legendary Birds of Kanto, along with Zapdos and Moltres.

  • Zapdos

    Zapdos is an Electric/Flying type Legendary Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. Zapdos has no known evolutionary line, and is one of the three Legendary Birds of Kanto, along with Articuno and Moltres.

  • Moltres

    Moltres is a Fire/Flying type Legendary Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. Moltres has no known evolutionary line, and is one of the three Legendary Birds of Kanto, along with Articuno and Zapdos.

  • Mewtwo

    Mewtwo is a Psychic type Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. Mewtwo has no known evolutionary line, but it can evolved into Mega Mewtwo X and Mega Mewtwo Y.

  • Mew

    Mew is a psychic type Mythical Pokemon introduced in Generation 1. Mew has no known evolutionary line. Mew has a unique ability to learn every TM and HM move, and most move tutor moves. Mew is the only Mythical Pokemon introduced in Generation 1 .
